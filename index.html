Our Profile

Founded with passion for speed and style, GarageTheftAuto is a trusted destination for car and motorcycle enthusiasts seeking not only quality vehicles but also a personal touch that brings their automotive dreams to life. Starting from a small workshop, we have grown into a reliable dealer and modification specialist for cars and bikes, focusing on delivering quality, performance, and style for every customer.

Our team consists of certified mechanics and experienced automotive enthusiasts dedicated to making your vehicle stand out while ensuring comfort and safety on the road. Whether you are looking to buy your first bike, upgrade your car, or transform your vehicle into a head-turning masterpiece, GarageTheftAuto is your trusted partner on the journey.

At Garage Theft Auto, we:

  • Sell new and used cars and motorcycles, ensuring each unit passes our strict quality standards.
  • Provide custom modification services for cars and bikes, including engine tuning, body kit installation, performance upgrades, and aesthetic customizations tailored to your lifestyle.
  • Guide customers in choosing the right vehicle and modification packages to match their daily needs or racing ambitions.
